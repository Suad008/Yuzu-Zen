Our Yuzu Zen Restuarant
Info.

Welcome to Yuzu Zen, where culinary artistry meets Zen tranquility.
Nestled in the heart of Baku, Yuzu Zen is a Japanese restaurant that invites you on a journey of exquisite flavors and serene ambiance.
The name itself reflects the essence of our culinary philosophy, with "Yuzu" representing the unique citrusy notes that punctuate our dishes, and "Zen" capturing the peaceful atmosphere we strive to create.
